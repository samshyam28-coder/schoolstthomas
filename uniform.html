LP Boys Section

The Lower Primary (LP) boys' uniform is designed for both comfort and a smart, academic look. This ensemble includes:

  • Regulation Shirt: A crisp light blue and white vertical striped half-sleeve shirt featuring a functional chest pocket.
  • Shorts: Tailored royal blue shorts made from durable, breathable fabric with front pleats for a formal finish.
  • Official Belt: The signature navy and white striped belt with the official school crest buckle.
  • Footwear: Paired with navy blue striped socks and polished black Velcro-strap shoes for ease of use and safety.
Standard Regulation

Lower Primary Girls

The Lower Primary uniform for girls is designed for comfort and ease of movement for younger students. This ensemble includes:

  • Pinafore: A sky blue pleated pinafore featuring the school emblem.
  • Striped Shirt: A matching blue and white striped half-sleeve shirt worn underneath.
  • Official Belt: A navy and white striped belt with the school's central crest.
  • Footwear: Paired with white socks and classic black Mary Jane style shoes.
Primary Regulation

UP & HS Boys Section

The Upper Primary and High School (UP & HS) boys' uniform transitions to a more formal academic look. This ensemble includes:

  • Formal Shirt: A crisp light blue and white vertical striped half-sleeve shirt with the school badge.
  • Trousers: Sharp royal blue full-length trousers tailored with durable, formal fabric.
  • Official Belt: The signature navy and white striped belt with the school crest buckle.
  • Footwear: Paired with navy striped socks and polished black formal lace-up or strap shoes.
Standard Regulation

UP & HS Girls Section

The Upper Primary and High School uniform for girls is designed for a professional and modest academic appearance. This ensemble includes:

  • Striped Shirt: A signature light blue and white striped half-sleeve shirt.
  • Official Waistcoat: A tailored royal blue button-up waistcoat featuring the school crest.
  • Trousers: Matching royal blue formal trousers designed for comfort and style.
  • Footwear: Paired with white socks and polished black formal lace-up shoes.
Standard Regulation

Higher Secondary Boys

The Higher Secondary boys' uniform introduces a sophisticated color palette for the senior years. This ensemble includes:

  • Lavender Shirt: A crisp lavender half-sleeve shirt featuring the school badge.
  • Grey Trousers: Tailored dark grey formal trousers for a sharp, senior look.
  • Signature Belt: The official striped navy and white belt with a gold-tone crest buckle.
  • Footwear: Paired with black socks and polished black formal lace-up shoes.
Senior Regulation

Higher Secondary Girls

The Higher Secondary girls' uniform features a sophisticated lavender and grey color palette, emphasizing a professional senior look. This ensemble includes:

  • Lavender Shirt: A crisp lavender half-sleeve shirt with a formal collar.
  • Official Waistcoat: A tailored grey button-up waistcoat featuring the school crest.
  • Trousers: Matching grey formal trousers designed for a sharp and comfortable fit.
  • Footwear: Paired with white socks and polished black formal lace-up shoes.
Senior Regulation
