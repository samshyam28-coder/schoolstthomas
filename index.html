WELCOME TO ST. THOMAS HSS, POONTHURA

A legacy of academic excellence and character building since 1910. Situated in the heart of the coastal community, St. Thomas HSS has been a beacon of knowledge, nurturing generations of students through a holistic approach to education. We are committed to fostering an environment where tradition meets modern innovation, empowering every child to lead with integrity, compassion, and the skillsets required for the future.

KNOW MORE

Our Vision

To be a premier institution that inspires academic excellence and character development, empowering every student to become a compassionate leader and a global citizen rooted in strong ethical values.

Our Mission

To provide a holistic and inclusive learning environment through the Kerala State Syllabus, fostering intellectual curiosity, creative thinking, and moral integrity while nurturing the unique talents of every child.
