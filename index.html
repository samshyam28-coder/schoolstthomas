Manager's Message

Welcome to St. Thomas. As the Manager of this esteemed institution, it is my pride to witness the growth of our students into responsible citizens. Our vision is to provide an environment where tradition meets modern innovation. We believe that true education goes beyond textbooks; it is about building character, fostering discipline, and instilling a sense of social responsibility.

We are committed to providing the best infrastructure and a supportive community for our students to thrive. Together with our dedicated faculty and supportive parents, we continue to strive for excellence in every endeavor.

Warm Regards,
Rev. Dr. Darwin Peter
The Manager

Principal's Message

At St. Thomas, we believe that every child is unique and possesses a special talent that needs to be nurtured. Our academic curriculum, based on the Kerala State Syllabus, is designed to challenge students intellectually while encouraging creative thinking. We don't just teach; we inspire.

In this rapidly changing world, our focus is on holistic development—balancing academics with sports, arts, and moral values. Our teachers act as mentors, guiding students through their journey of discovery and helping them unlock their full potential. I invite you to be a part of our vibrant learning community.

Sincerely,
Mr. Thadeus
The Principal

Headmaster's Message

The foundation of a successful life is laid in the classrooms of a school. As the Headmaster, my priority is to ensure a safe, disciplined, and happy environment for all our students. We focus heavily on the fundamentals of learning, ensuring that every child receives the individual attention they deserve.

We take pride in our extracurricular achievements and our consistent academic results. By working closely with parents, we aim to shape students who are not only smart but also kind and hardworking. Let us work together to give our children the bright future they deserve.

Best Wishes,
Mr. Thadhayoose Remias
The Headmaster
